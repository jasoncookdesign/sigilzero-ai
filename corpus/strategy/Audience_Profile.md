# Audience Profile

SIGIL.ZERO serves a literate underground audience.

Depth over hype.  
Structure over trend.

---

## Promoters / Bookers

- Seek reliability and system-ready sound.  
- Value discipline and consistency.  
- Prioritize catalog coherence.

---

## DJs / Selectors

- Require mixable structure.  
- Trust imprint-level quality control.  
- Seek energy predictability without monotony.

---

## Listeners

- Prefer discovery over marketing.  
- Respond to symbolic and aesthetic cohesion.  
- Value archive over algorithm.

---

The audience rewards restraint and consistency.
